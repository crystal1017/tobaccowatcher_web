What We Do

Tobacco Watcher brings together disparate news sources in multiple languages, analyzes the content of these sources using modern natural language processing techniques, and visualizes and disseminates the results of this analysis for advocates, policy makers, and students alike.

Using Tobacco Watcher you will be able to filter alerts about the changing tobacco control landscape by your geographic (i.e., country) or topical (i.e., taxation) interest. Running live 24/7/365, the system will equip you with the necessary understanding, day or night, to rid the globe of smoking.
